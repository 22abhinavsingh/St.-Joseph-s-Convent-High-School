OUR ORIGIN

The Congregation of the Sisters of St. Joseph of the Apparition was founded in Gaillac,France,on Christmas day,1832by Emilie de Vialar who is now a canonized saint.

St. Joseph's Convent High School,Mosaboni Mines was established in 1960 by the diocese of Jamshedpur,and handed over to the sisters of St. Joseph of the Apparition in 1966.It has a branch School in Moubhandar.

Our Motto : ONWARD WITH HONOUR

The motto of the school should inspire everyone in our lifes, at home, in school and in all dealings with others.

The four symbolof the Emblem are as follows:

1-FLEUR DELYS: National flower of france,a reminder of the origin.

2-S.J.: Stands for St. Joseph,Patron of the Congregation and of the School.He is also our guardian and guide.

3-LIGHT: We are children of the light and our light should shine and act as a beacon for others.

4-LOTUS: National flower of India: A symbol of all that is beautiful and this should be reflected in our lives.
